The creature is tall and whispy, with a reptilian features on it's face and across
most of it's body.  It is very fleet of foot and dexterous.  As you look closer you
notice very detailed facial paint and tattoos.  When in communicates you hear nothing
but a series of clicks and hisses.  This particular one is clothed in animal hides and
has feathers of a myriad of colors jutting out from its matted brownish black hair.
