One of the most important aspects of this stronghold will be how much revenue
it can drive to you as its owner.  This particular mountain we find ourselves
in is laden full of rich minerals and metals that can be mined and turned to 
gold pieces.  So, with that said, we must build a Gold mine.  Do so and you
will reap rewards.

{gObjectives:{n
    -Build a Gold Mine Structure

Use the @construct command while in your lair to build a Gold Mine structure.
type help @construct for more information.
