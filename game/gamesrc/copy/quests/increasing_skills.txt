To become the most proficient General in the land, training is paramount!  Each you skill you learn
can be improved by spending your accrued experience on it.  Each point in the skill increases numerous
statistics about it including: chance to hit, damage amount, buff amount etc.  Each skill is able to be
increased until level 100.

To view the skill upgrage menu simply use to 'skills command'.  Example: skills

Once you open the skills menu, it will be very easy to figure out how to increase said skills.


    {GObjectives{n:
        - Increase the rank of one skill.

Increase a skills rank.
