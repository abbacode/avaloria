Another important thing to learn is how to acquire new skills.  This is fairly simple
in the land of Avaloria.  Simply find, or purchase the appropriate training manual for
the desired skill and then use the 'use' command to read the book and learn the skill.
For example: use Training Manual

Once you do this the skill will be added to your known skills list and you will be able
to spend experience points to increase the skill level of your known skills.  You can
do this with the 'skills' command.  Take this training manual near me and learn the skill
is teaches.
