One of the most important things that you can learn is how to communicate with others.  There
is an easy way to do this within the realm of Avaloria.  This can be accomplished by using the
'ooc' command.  All you must do is type: ooc message you want you send.

Try it.  Say something. The channel you speak on can be heard by all the other generals in the 
land.  So perhaps you want to be courteous and nice when you communicate, some may even offer to
help you.
