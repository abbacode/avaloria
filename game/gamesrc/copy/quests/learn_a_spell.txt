Magic is abundant in Avaloria.  Most inhabitants and creatures posess innate magical ability.
All one must do is find a spell tome for the appropriate spell and use it to learn the knowledge
stored within.  Magic spells use up a designated amount of your mana upon each cast.  If you
can not affored to cast the spell, it will fail.

On the ground next to me you will find a spell tome.  Pick it up with the 'get' command and then
read it by using the 'use' command.  Example: use spell tome

Once you read and retain the knowledge, you will be able to view the spell in your spellbook by
issues the command: show spells
